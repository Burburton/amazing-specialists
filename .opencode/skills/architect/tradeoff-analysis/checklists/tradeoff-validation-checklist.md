# Tradeoff Validation Checklist

**Skill**: `tradeoff-analysis`  
**Purpose**: Validate trade-off analysis is complete and actionable

---

## Pre-Conditions

- [ ] Decision point identified
- [ ] Multiple alternatives available
- [ ] Constraints understood

---

## Alternatives Evaluation

- [ ] At least 2 alternatives documented
- [ ] Each alternative has pros listed
- [ ] Each alternative has cons listed
- [ ] Alternatives are genuinely different approaches
- [ ] Status quo considered as alternative (if applicable)

---

## Decision Quality

- [ ] Selected approach explicitly stated
- [ ] Selection rationale is specific
- [ ] Rejected approaches have explicit reasons
- [ ] Decision is not arbitrary

---

## Trade-off Documentation

- [ ] Trade-offs are explicit (what gained vs. what sacrificed)
- [ ] Trade-off rationale explains why acceptable
- [ ] Both benefits AND costs documented
- [ ] Language is specific, not vague

---

## Risk Assessment

- [ ] At least 2 risks identified
- [ ] Each risk has severity level
- [ ] Each risk has mitigation strategy
- [ ] High-severity risks have detailed mitigations

---

## Revisit Trigger

- [ ] Revisit trigger is defined
- [ ] Trigger is specific and measurable
- [ ] Trigger includes quantitative thresholds
- [ ] Trigger is realistic to monitor

---

## Anti-Pattern Check

- [ ] **NOT** decision without alternatives
- [ ] **NOT** vague language (specifics used)
- [ ] **NOT** missing costs (both pros and cons)
- [ ] **NOT** missing revisit trigger

---

## Downstream Consumability

- [ ] Developer understands why approach chosen
- [ ] Reviewer can evaluate decision quality
- [ ] Security can assess risk implications
- [ ] Future maintainers understand context

---

## Post-Conditions

- [ ] `risks-and-tradeoffs` artifact follows AC-003 contract
- [ ] All 7 required fields present
- [ ] Decision is defensible with documented rationale
